Why Choose Us...

ICSC was established in 1961 by the State Govt. as an autonomous educational institute to offer higher education at both undergraduate and postgraduate levels in various disciplines of science and management studies and evolved as one of the notable glorious seat of academic excellence and grandeur of the state. The academic credentials and the prestige of the College in 2010 attracted the attention of University Grants Commission, New Delhi and the College was granted CPE status (College with Potential for Excellence) the only College in J & K State.


      The College has been declared as an Autonomous College under section 2(b-a) of the Kashmir and Jammu Universities Act No. XXIV of 1969 (as amended in 2014) for a period of six years (2014-15 To 2019-2020) under UGC Notification No.F.22-1/2014(AC) dated 03-08-2014 followed by University Notification No.F(Auto-Status-ICSC)Acad/KU/14 Dated 13-10-2014. Recognizing all these credentials of the College the National Assessment and Accreditation council Bangalore (NAAC) Re-accredited this College at "A" level with institutional score as 3.27 CGPA in august, 2017.
